01 · Who we are

From soil to ship — a single, honest promise.

Gosarvam Global LLP is an Indian export house specialising in premium agri, eco, and food essentials. We work with farmer cooperatives and family-run producers across India to bring authentic, sustainable, export-grade products to international buyers.

What began as a small initiative to bring Assam's finest teas to the world has grown into a curated portfolio of seven flagship categories — each anchored in tradition, processed with modern rigour, and shipped with full traceability.

02 · Our North Star

Mission

Deliver authentic, eco-friendly and culturally rich products globally — while empowering farmers, supporting sustainable trade, and elevating the perception of Indian craft.

  • Empower farmers with fair pricing, training, and long-term offtake.
  • Maintain authenticity with single-origin traceability per consignment.
  • Lead on sustainability across packaging, energy, and logistics.

Vision

"Connecting India's soil with the world's future."

Within a decade, Gosarvam Global aims to be the most recognised Indian export brand for premium agri and eco products — synonymous with quality, integrity, and the elevation of the producers we partner with.
