Privacy Policy

Last updated · 27 May 2026

Gosarvam Global LLP ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Policy explains what information we collect when you interact with our website, products, and trade services, and how we use, store, and protect it.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact information — name, company, email, phone, country — provided when you submit RFQs, catalogue requests, or contact forms.
  • Business information — trade preferences, quantities, ports, payment terms — shared during sales conversations.
  • Technical information — browser type, IP address, pages viewed, anonymised analytics.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to RFQs, send quotations, ship catalogues and samples.
  • To comply with export, customs and tax obligations.
  • To improve the website, products, and trade workflow.
  • For occasional, opt-in newsletters and trade briefs.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your information. We share data only with logistics partners, banks, regulatory authorities, and processors when required to fulfil a transaction or a legal obligation.

4. Cookies & analytics

We use minimal, privacy-respecting cookies for site analytics. You can opt out via your browser settings without losing access to the site's core features.

5. Data retention

We retain commercial records as required by Indian export regulations (typically 7 years). General contact and inquiry data is retained for 24 months unless you ask us to delete it sooner.

6. Your rights

You may at any time request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing privacy@gosarvamglobal.com.

7. Security

We use TLS encryption, restricted access, and secure document storage. No transmission is ever 100% secure, but we apply industry best practice across our systems.

8. Updates

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@gosarvamglobal.com.
