RFQ received

Docs to buyer

Step 10 · Delivery

Vessel sails

02 · Shipping Modes

Right mode, right time, right cost.

FCL

Full Container Load

20ft / 40ft / 40HC. Lowest per-unit cost. Ideal for volumes above 15 tonnes.

LCL

Less than Container Load

Shared container space. Cost-effective for 2–15 tonnes. Consolidation through major ports.

AIR

Air Freight

Fastest mode (3–5 days). Best for premium / time-sensitive goods. Multiple airline partners.

DDP

Door-Delivery

End-to-end DAP / DDP for select markets. We handle customs, last-mile, all-inclusive.

EXP

Express Courier

For samples and small consignments — DHL / FedEx / Aramex air courier worldwide.

REF

Reefer Container

Temperature-controlled containers for sensitive goods. -25°C to +25°C settings.

03 · Payment Terms

Flexible, secure, internationally accepted.

We work with all common international payment instruments — agreed upfront in the proforma invoice and locked in writing.

Most popular

TT · Telegraphic Transfer

30% advance on PO. 70% against scanned BL copy. Direct bank wire — fastest, lowest fees.

Trusted

LC · Letter of Credit

Irrevocable LC at sight from a prime bank. 100% covered on shipment + compliant docs.

Established

DA / DP

Documents against acceptance / payment for repeat buyers with established credit history.

Modern

USDT / Crypto

For select buyers — USDT on-chain settlement. Faster than wire, locked at PI rate.

Insured

ECGC Cover

Export credit insurance via ECGC India for select buyers and large-volume PO terms.

Currencies

USD · EUR · GBP · AED

Multi-currency banking. Pricing locked in your preferred currency at PI confirmation.
