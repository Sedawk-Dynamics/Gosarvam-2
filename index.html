03 · The Journey

From soil to ship — every step, by design.

CHAPTER 01

The Garden, before the cup.

Our Assam estates sit between the Brahmaputra and the foothills of the Himalayas — a microclimate that has produced legendary tea for over 180 years. We work with second-generation tea families who hand-pluck only the top two leaves and a bud.

  • Hand-plucked — only two leaves and a bud, twice daily.
  • Orthodox processing — withered, rolled, oxidised, fired traditionally.
  • Single-origin lots — full estate traceability per consignment.
Assam · 2200 ft elevation
CHAPTER 02

Farmer-first sourcing.

Over 450 farming families form the backbone of every shipment. We pay above-market premium, fund agronomy training, and invest in shared cold-chain and drying infrastructure. Empowerment is the only sustainable supply chain.

  • Above-market fair-trade pricing.
  • Annual agronomy and organic-conversion training.
  • Shared warehouses, dryers, and grading lines.
450+ farmer partners
CHAPTER 03

Export-grade by default.

Every consignment passes triple-quality inspection — at farm, at processing, and at the port. ISO-certified packing lines, vacuum-sealed export grades, multilingual labeling, and full HS-code-ready documentation.

  • Triple QC — farm, processing, port.
  • HACCP & FSSAI compliant facilities.
  • FCL / LCL / Air — flexible global logistics.
